Welcome to MINA | MAP INDIAN ARCHAEOLOGY

MINA is a digital Web platform of archaeological investigations in post-colonial India (currently 1953-1961). Produced as capstone for the Institute on Digital Archaeology Methods and Practices, the platform aims to promote scholarly interest in the archaeology of India and neighbouring South Asian nations and to encourage students to employ digital methods in archaeology.
